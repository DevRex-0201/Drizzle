### Neon 
Neon database due to their [official website](https://neon.tech) - is a multi-cloud fully managed Postgres. 
This docs section is for Neon Serverless [[github](https://github.com/neondatabase/serverless), [blog post](https://blog.cloudflare.com/neon-postgres-database-from-workers/)], 
their driver to access Neon from a serverless environments with no TCP available, through websockets.  
  
If you're about to use Neon from a serverfull environments, due to their [official nodejs docs](https://neon.tech/docs/guides/node) 
you should just use PostgresJS driver - see [docs](./postgresjs).
